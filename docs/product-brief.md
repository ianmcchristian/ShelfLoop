# ShelfLoop Product Brief

## Objective

Demonstrate how RFID-enabled apparel fixtures can close the loop between sales-floor availability, backroom stock, and guided replenishment work.

## Primary users

- Store associate / stocker: receives and completes replenishment tasks.
- Store manager: monitors inventory health, exceptions, and service level.
- Product/business stakeholder: evaluates operational impact and deployment tradeoffs.

## MVP scenario

A men's apparel display falls below its replenishment threshold. ShelfLoop simulates an RFID scan, identifies the missing quantity, validates backroom availability, creates a shared replenishment task, and guides the stocker from bin to fixture with pulsing tap-to-light cues.

## Success signals

- Missing inventory can be detected from scan events.
- Backroom stock determines whether a restock task or out-of-stock state is created.
- The dashboard communicates operational state in under two minutes.
- The stocker workflow shows a concrete end-to-end replenishment loop.
